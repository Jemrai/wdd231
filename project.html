Individual Project Site Plan: Real Estate Club

Site Name

Urban Realty Network

This name represents a club focused on real estate professionals and enthusiasts in urban areas, emphasizing networking and community.

Optional domain availability: urbanrealtynetwork.com (assumed available for this plan).

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for the Real Estate Club, providing information on membership, events, market trends, property listings, and resources for real estate professionals and investors. It includes forms for joining, event registrations, and educational content to foster networking and knowledge sharing in the real estate industry.

Scenarios

  • What are the current real estate market trends in urban areas, and how can I stay updated?
  • How do I join the Real Estate Club and access exclusive member benefits like networking events?

Color Scheme

The color scheme uses blue (#0066cc) for headings and links to convey trust and professionalism, often associated with real estate. Green (#32cd32) is used for accents, borders, and subheadings to represent growth and success in the industry. These colors will be applied to backgrounds, text, and UI elements throughout the site.

Typography

Primary font: Arial (sans-serif) for body text to ensure readability on all devices. Secondary font: Georgia (serif) for headings to add a professional and elegant touch. This combination keeps the site clean and accessible.
